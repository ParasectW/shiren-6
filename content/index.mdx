This is a wiki for Shiren the Wanderer: The Mystery Dungeon of Serpentcoil Island (Shiren 6).<br/>It's a work-in-progress, so some pages don't have content yet, and others may change in time.

If you spot any errors or wish to contribute, please post on [/r/ShirenTheWanderer](https://reddit.com/r/ShirenTheWanderer)<br/>or join the community [Discord server](https://discord.gg/5y7UDFc) and ping shark.

Of course, you're welcome to join just to hang out with other players and ROM hackers too.

A portion of the information found on this site is hand-translated from the [Japanese wiki](https://shiren6.game-info.wiki/).
